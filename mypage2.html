Burj Khalifa: The Icon of Dubai

Overview:

Standing at 828 meters, the Burj Khalifa is the tallest building in the world. Completed in 2010, this architectural marvel symbolizes Dubaiâ€™s ambitious goals. Visitors can marvel at panoramic views of the city from the observation deck or enjoy a meal at one of the high-end restaurants located within the tower.

Historical Significance:

The Burj Khalifa was designed by renowned architect Adrian Smith of Skidmore, Owings & Merrill. It took over six years to complete, with the construction being one of the largest engineering feats of the 21st century. It has become a symbol of Dubai's rapid development.

Features of Burj Khalifa:

  2. Floors: 163
  3. Observation Deck: 148th floor
  4. lighting: Over 4,000 LED lights
  5. Construction Time: 6 years
